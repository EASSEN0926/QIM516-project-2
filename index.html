Campus Malay for real student survival.

A practical Malay learning website for new international students in Malaysia. Every phrase is shown in Malay, English, and Chinese, with recorded Malay audio, scenario dialogue, quizzes, a searchable phrase bank, and progress tracking.

Why this application exists

International students often need Malay outside the classroom.

The application focuses on campus contexts: canteens, offices, clinics, directions, transportation, and emergency help. It is designed for short, practical learning instead of heavy textbook study.

😟

Communication anxiety

New students may avoid daily situations because they are afraid of misunderstanding others.

🎯

Contextual learning

Each lesson is built around real campus tasks, not random vocabulary lists.

🎧

Pronunciation support

Each phrase includes recorded Malay audio to help learners practise pronunciation more accurately.
